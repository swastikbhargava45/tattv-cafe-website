A Sanctuary for Creativity & Connection

At Tattv Art Cafe & Library, we believe that everyone has an artist inside them waiting to be discovered. We aren't just a place to grab coffee; we are Bhopal's premier destination for creative expression.

Whether you're molding clay at our pottery wheels, brushing colors onto a fresh canvas, or getting lost in a good book from our library, our space is designed to inspire. We host guided workshops that turn ordinary afternoons into unforgettable artistic journeys.
